German Shepherds

Fun facts

  • They were the first breed used as guide dogs
  • German sheperds are considered very intelligent dogs, they are the third smartest breed, and are used for various security and medical purposes
  • They are currently being trained to sniff out COVID-19 to be used in airports
  • They are often used as police dogs
